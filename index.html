Biography

Welcome to my homepage. I received my B.E. and Ph.D. degrees from the Electronic Engineering Department at Tsinghua University, China in 2010 and 2015, respectively. My advisor was Professor Zhisheng Niu from Niulab. I visited the WiDeS group at University of Southern California, USA from 2013 to 2014, advised by Professor Andreas F. Molisch. I then worked as an experienced researcher at Ericsson from 2015 to 2016. I visited ARNG at University of Southern California, USA, working with Professor Bhaskar Krishnamachari from 2017 to 2018. I worked as a wireless signal processing scientist at Intel Labs, Hillsboro, USA during 2018. I’m now working as Professor of the School of Communications and Information Engineering (SCIE) at Shanghai University, China. For my research, please visit the ACELab homepage.
